About Tehila International Logistics

Built on Integrity, Driven by Excellence

Tehila International Logistics Ltd is a Uganda-based clearing and forwarding company operating from Nakawa and Entebbe — Uganda's gateway to global trade. Founded with a commitment to transparency, compliance and client success, we have built strong partnerships with URA, freight forwarders, and importers across Uganda and the East African Community (EAC).

Led by Director Fred Rwakyooya, a seasoned logistics professional with deep expertise in URA customs procedures, ASYCUDA World declarations, cross-border trade and regional logistics networks, our team brings real-world experience and unwavering dedication to every shipment.

How It Works

Our Clearance Process

A proven, step-by-step approach to getting your cargo cleared and delivered without delays.

01
📄
Submit Documents

Provide commercial invoice, packing list, Bill of Lading / Airway Bill and permits. We handle the rest.

02
🖥️
ASYCUDA Filing

We lodge your declaration on ASYCUDA World, applying correct HS codes and EAC CET duty rates for full URA compliance.

03
✅
Customs Release

After assessment and duty payment, we obtain the release order and supervise physical examination where required.

04
🚛
Delivery & Handover

Your cargo is transported to your specified destination and handed over with full documentation and delivery confirmation.
